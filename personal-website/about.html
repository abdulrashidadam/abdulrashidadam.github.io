City Planner Land Use Planner Environmental Planner Transportation Planner

I am an urban and regional planner who specializes in land use, transportation, and environmental systems and how they affect the way in which cities and regions operate. My work involves planning, which can be understood in two different ways: one involves the technical aspect, and the other involves the ethical aspect of planning. My belief in planning involves creating and improving access to essential services in a way that improves the quality of life in communities. Currently, I am a Master of Science in Urban and Regional Planning candidate at the University of Iowa, specializing in land use and environmental planning.

My academic experience includes training in land use law, environmental and transportation planning, and research methods, including spatial analysis using Geographic Information Systems. In addition, I am currently working on a comprehensive update of the City of West Branch, Iowa, and am a co-editor of this project and a liaison coordinating interdisciplinary teams on planning, housing, historic preservation, and capital improvement projects. My experience includes work in land use planning, transportation mapping, environmental sustainability, and data-driven planning in various capacities, including the internship experience in which I developed a transportation routing web application and created housing mapping tools for the Housing Fellowship in Iowa City, Iowa. My interests in planning involve positions in land use planning, environmental planning, transportation planning, and city planning in which I can contribute to open and participatory planning processes for sustainable community development.
